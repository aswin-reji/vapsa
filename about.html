About Us

VAPSA: Your Trusted Partner

Vapsa was founded in 2019 by a group of engineers having immense experience in diverse fields of engineering such as mechanical, electrical, instrumentation and automation in various sectors. Our primary objective is to maintain VAPSA as an organization that caters comprehensively to the needs and challenges faced by industrial customers. Over the years VAPSA has emerged as a trusted partner for delivering EPC (Engineering, Procurement and Construction) solutions to industries such as Oil & Gas/ power/ Food processing/ Pharma and Renewable Energy.


What sets us apart is our teams diverse expertise gained through extensive exposure to international clients and challenging projects. We believe in maintaining strong relationships with our customers and ensuring their utmost satisfaction. Our customer centric approach coupled with our commitment to excellence positions us as a reliable and trusted partner for all your engineering needs.
